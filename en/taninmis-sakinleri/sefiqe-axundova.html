Shafiqa Akhundova

Shafiqa Akhundova (January 21, 1924, Nukha - July 26, 2013, Baku) - Azerbaijani composer, People's Artist of the Republic of Azerbaijan (1998), laureate of the Order of Glory (2005). She is considered to be the first woman composer to write an opera in the East. Shafiqa Akhundova was born in 1924 in the city of Sheki.
