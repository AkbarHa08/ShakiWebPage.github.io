Majid Rasulov

Majid Latif oglu Rasulov, a graduate of the Azerbaijan State Pedagogical Institute and a post-graduate student of the Azerbaijan State University (now BSU), created his own scientific direction, a prominent specialist in mathematical physics equations, worked in fields such as differential equations and functional analysis. is one of the personalities. His research can be divided into four areas: The first area is his work on the theory of special derivative differential equations, where he gives the methods of derivation and contour integration for solving large class boundary, mixed and Cauchy problems. The work in the field of spectral theory of linear differential operators can be attributed to the second direction. Here he is proved the new separation formulas for the solution of spectral problems for contour integrals and differential equations of arbitrary vector-function. applying the analysis to the theory of differential operators obtained the normal condition of the ordinary linear differential operator.
