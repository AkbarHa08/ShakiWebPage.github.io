Elnur Iskandarov

Elnur Iskandar oglu Iskandarov (June 1, 1989; Sheki, Azerbaijan - April 2, 2016; Azerbaijan) - Officer (senior lieutenant) of the Azerbaijani Armed Forces. He was killed during the fighting in April 2016 and was posthumously awarded the Medal for the Fatherland. Elnur Isgandarov was born on June 1, 1989 in the city of Sheki. In 2006, Elnur Isgandarov entered the Azerbaijan Higher Military School named after Heydar Aliyev, and in 2010 he graduated with the rank of lieutenant. Later, Elnur Isgandarov began to serve in the special forces unit of the military unit "N" located in Baku. Elnur Isgandarov was married, he has a son named Elman (d. 2015).
