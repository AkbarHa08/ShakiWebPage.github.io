Shirmammad Huseynov

Shirmammad Agamammad oglu Huseynov (December 17, 1924, Nukha - June 24, 2019, Baku) - a prominent scientist and media researcher of Azerbaijan, laureate of the Supreme Media Award, Presidential Fellow, Professor of the Department of Press History and Ideological Methods of Baku State University. Shirmammad Huseynov was born in 1924 in Sheki. He graduated from high school in 1940. In 1941-1943 he entered the physics and mathematics faculty of Sheki Teachers' Institute. During World War II, he worked as a teacher in Zagatala and Sheki. In 1945-1950 he graduated from the journalism department of the philological faculty of Baku State University. In 1950-1954 he continued his postgraduate studies at the journalism faculty of the Moscow State University named after MVLomonosov. MSU in 1954, He defended his dissertation at the Faculty of Journalism. From 1954 to 1961 he worked as a teacher, associate professor at the Department of Journalism, Faculty of Philology, BSU. In 1962-1969 he worked at BSU, Deputy Dean of the Faculty of Philology, in 1961-1971 at BSU, Associate Professor of the Department of Theory and Practice of Journalism, and in 1971-1976 at the BSU, Dean of the Faculty of Journalism. In 1971-1988 he was an associate professor of the department of press history at BSU, in 1976-1988 he was the head of the department of press history at BSU, and since 1988 he has been a professor at the department of press history at BSU.
