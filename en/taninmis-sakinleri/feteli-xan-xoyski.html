Fatali Khan Khoyski

Fatalikhan Iskander oglu Khoyski or Fatali khan Khoyski or Fatali khan Khoyslu - Azerbaijani political figure, deputy of the II State Duma of the Russian Empire, People's Commissar of Education under the Transcaucasian Commissariat (1917–1918), Minister of Justice of the Transcaucasian Democratic Federal Republic (1918). After the proclamation of the Azerbaijan Democratic Republic, he was appointed Chairman of the First Council of Ministers (1918–1919) and Minister of the Interior (1918), as well as Minister of Justice (1918) and Minister of Foreign Affairs (1918–1919 and 1919–1920). .
