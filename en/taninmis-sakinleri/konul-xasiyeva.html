Konul Khasiyeva

Konul Khasiyeva was born on March 17, 1968 in Sheki. Konul Khasiyeva entered the first grade in 1975. In 1981, he won the "National Youth Review" competition organized by the order of the 3rd President of the Republic of Azerbaijan Heydar Aliyev. In 1981-1986 he was a student of People's Artist Shovkat Alakbarova at the Bulbul Music School. In 1986-1991 he studied at the Azerbaijan State Conservatory (now the Academy of Music) and was a student of People's Artist Arif Babayev.
