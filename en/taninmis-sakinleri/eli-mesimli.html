Ali Masimli

Ali Ahmad oglu Masimov was born on January 3, 1953 in the city of Sheki. He graduated from the faculty of political economy of the Azerbaijan Institute of National Economy. He is a candidate of economic sciences. He is the author of numerous brochures and articles. He speaks Russian. Since 1980, he worked as a laboratory assistant, junior researcher, researcher, department head at the Institute of Economics of the Academy of Sciences of Azerbaijan. Since 1991, Head of the Department of Economic Management of the Office of the President of the Republic of Azerbaijan, Chief Adviser of the Department of Socio-Economic Development, Chairman of the Committee on Foreign Investments of the Republic of Azerbaijan since 1992, First Deputy Prime Minister, Chairman of the State Committee for Economy and Planning. Deputy Minister, Minister of Economy, 1993-2005 "
