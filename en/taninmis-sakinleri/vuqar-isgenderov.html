Vugar Isgandarov

Vugar Japon oglu Iskandarov was born on December 30, 1972 in the city of Sheki. In 1979, he entered the first grade of secondary school No. 17 in Sheki, and in 1989 he graduated from the 10th grade of that school. He served in the military in 1991-1992. He is married. He has 2 children. Vugar Iskandarov entered the law faculty of the Scientific-Educational Institute of the Republic of Georgia in 1996, and graduated from the same institute in 2001 with a degree in law. He received a special education in public administration in the United States in 2008. In 2012, he entered the Faculty of Economics of the Azerbaijan State Agrarian University, and in 2014 he graduated from the same university with a degree in economics. Vugar Iskandarov has been practicing taekwondo since 1990, and karate-do since 1992. In 1993-1995 he worked as a karate-do coach, in 1995 he was appointed chairman of the Association of the Azerbaijan Karate-do Federation in the north-western region. From September 6, 1995 to September 2, 1998 he was a chief engineer for technical safety at the Independent Transport Union, from September 6, 1998 to April 22, 1999 he was an economist at the Sheki 62 Repair and Construction Department, 1999 From April 1, 2001 to January 3, 2000 he worked as a leading lawyer in the Union of Cooperatives of Sheki region, from February 7, 2001 to December 26, 2005 he worked as a legal adviser of Sheki MRC. In 2001, he was appointed chairman of the north-western region of the Azerbaijan Taekwondo Federation. He has been a trainer in Leadership and Time Management programs. Since that year he has been a member of the Azerbaijani national karate-do team. In 2001, Vugar Iskandarov established a special school on oriental martial arts for the first time among the regions of the republic. He has represented Azerbaijan in sports and other fields in about 40 countries around the world. Vugar Iskandarov was appointed the head of Sheki Youth and Sports Department on December 29, 2005. He was the Deputy Chairman of the Sheki Organization of the New Azerbaijan Party, a member of the Council under the Sheki City Executive Power, and in 2010-2015 he was the Chairman of the State Examination Commission of the Sheki Pedagogical College. In 2020, he was elected a deputy of the Milli Majlis of the VI convocation.
