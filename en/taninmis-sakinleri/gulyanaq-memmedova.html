Gulyanag Mammadova

Mammadova Gulyanag Zakir gizi was born on June 10, 1973 in Boyuk Dahna village of Sheki region. In 1980-1990 in Boyuk Dahna secondary school No. 1, in 1992-1996 in the mugam department of the Music College under the Azerbaijan National Conservatory (former name Music College named after A.Zeynalli), in 1996-2000 Azerbaijan State Culture and Art He studied acting at the "Musical Theater and Film Actor" faculty of the University. He graduated from the university in 2002 with a master's degree. In 1990-1992 he worked in the House of Culture of Sheki region. Since 1993 he has been working as a vocalist at the Azerbaijan State Song Theater named after R. Behbudov. In 1995-1997, he performed at the Malmo Festival in Malmo, Sweden, and gave concerts in Stockholm and Coteburg. In 2000, he gave concerts at EXPO-2000 in Honnover, Germany, and in 2000 at the Bogazici Festival in Turkey in Istanbul and Ankara. In the concert program of the propaganda group of the Baku-Tbilisi-Ceyhan oil pipeline in 2001-2002, on the occasion of the 1st anniversary of Azerbaijan's accession to the Council of Europe in Strasbourg, France in 2002; In 2002 in Vienna, Austria, Moscow, Kiev, Ukraine; In the "Days of Azerbaijani Culture" in Moscow in 2002-2003; In Ukraine in 2002; In Moscow and Minsk in 2003; In 2003 at the "Days of Azerbaijani Culture" in Mainz, Germany; In Yekaterinburg in 2004 in connection with Novruz Holiday in 2005 in Chisinau, Moldova; In 2005 at the "Days of Azerbaijani Culture" in Vienna, Austria; In 2006, he was on tour in Nakhchivan in connection with the "State Independence Day". In 1998, he was awarded the title of Honored Artist of the Republic of Azerbaijan. He was awarded the Presidential Award on May 9, 2012.
