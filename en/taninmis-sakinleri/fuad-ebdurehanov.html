Fuad Abdurrahmanov

Fuad Hasan oglu Abdurrahmanov (April 28, 1915, Nukha, Yelizavetpol province - June 15, 1971, Baku) - sculptor, People's Artist of the Azerbaijan SSR (1955), corresponding member of the USSR Academy of Arts (1949), winner of the Stalin Prize (1947, 1951). Fuad Abdurrahmanov was born on May 11, 1915 in Nukha (now the city of Sheki). He studied at the Azerbaijan State Art College (1929-1932) and the Leningrad Institute of Painting, Sculpture and Architecture named after IY Repin (1935-1939). He taught at the Azerbaijan State Art School named after Azimzade.
