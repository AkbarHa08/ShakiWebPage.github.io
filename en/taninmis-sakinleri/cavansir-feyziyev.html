Javanshir Feyziyev

Javanshir Feyziyev was born in 1963 in Boyuk Dahna village of Nukha region. In 1970–1977 he received his secondary education at the Boyuk Dahna village secondary school, and in 1977–1980 at the Turan boarding school with a mathematical orientation.
1983–1985 Served in the army in Kazakhstan.
In 1986-1991 he studied at the translation faculty of the Azerbaijan Pedagogical Institute of Foreign Languages ​​and graduated from the institute with honors in translation-teaching.
In 1992-1994 he worked as a teacher at the translation department of the Azerbaijan Pedagogical Institute of Foreign Languages.
From 1994 to 1995, he worked as a translator and administrator for the Belgian humanitarian organization Doctors Without Borders.
In 1995–1998, Philip Morris of the United States Philip Morris worked in the Caucasus office for Azerbaijan.
In 1998–2002, ing. «Planet Co. Ltd. was the founder and director of the company.
From 2002 to 2010 he was a co-founder and CEO of EuroMed. ing. Tour-Invest Company and ing. He is the founder and editor-in-chief of the scientific-popular magazine "Discovery Azerbaijan".
In 2004-2009 he was a dissertation student of the Institute of Philosophy, Sociology and Law of the Azerbaijan National Academy of Sciences.
2010 - Elected to the Milli Majlis of the Republic of Azerbaijan of the 4th convocation from the second constituency No. 115 of Sheki village.
2011 - Doctoral student of the Institute of Philosophy, Sociology and Law of the National Academy of Sciences of the Republic of Azerbaijan.
2013 - Elected a member of the Board of the Press Council of the Republic of Azerbaijan.
2015 - Elected to the Milli Majlis of the Republic of Azerbaijan of the 5th convocation from the second constituency No. 115 of Sheki village.
