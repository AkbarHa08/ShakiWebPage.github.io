Sheki Cuisine

Sheki Cuisine Is As Rich As All Azerbaijani Cuisines. Sheki Piti, Sheki Halva (Baklava), Which Resonates All Over The World, Is A Proof Of This. The Main Dish Of Sheki Cuisine Is Sheki Piti. Other Dishes Of Sheki Cuisine Include Balva, Surfullu, Ballibadi, Sheki Carving (Known Locally As Oma), Girmabadam, Tel Halva, Rice Halva, Tutlu-Sudlu Pilaf, Chicken Pilaf, Etc. Can Be Named.

Popular Dishes

Piti

It Is Prepared As An Ordinary Piti. But Instead Of Potatoes, Chestnuts Are Taken. Chestnuts Are Cooked And Cleaned Separately. In Autumn, 30 G Of Quince Is Added To Each Stake. Sumac And Peeled Onions Are Served Next To The Pita.

Şəki Halvası

It is a confectionery product typical of Sheki region of the Republic of Azerbaijan. Ingredients: rice flour, water, hazelnuts or walnuts, powdered sugar and coriander seeds.

Tel Halva

It is a dessert typical of Sheki cuisine. It is similar to Pishmaniye, which belongs to the Turkish city of Izmit.

Balva

National dish of Azerbaijan. Cook the rice for 30 minutes, add finely chopped greens, flour soaked in water, and when ready, add butter, soaked lavash, salt, pepper and egg whites.

Sürfüllü

It is a pastry belonging to the north-western zone of Azerbaijan, mainly to Yeni Suvagil Gakh, Zagatala and Balakan regions, and partly to Guba-Khachmaz and Ganjabasar regions.

Oyma

It is one of the national sweets typical of Sheki region of Azerbaijan.
